About Me

I'm a computer science student specializing in mobile application development, currently working toward my engineering degree. As a vegan, I cook exclusively plant-based dishes. I'm inspired by Italian cuisine and culture, and I'm currently learning the Italian language.

I have acting experience in episodic roles in Polish TV series filmed in Wrocław, including Policjanci i Policjantki, Lombard. Życie pod zastaw, Święty, and Uroczysko. I've appeared in an internal ad for Kaufland and worked as a stand-in on the set of The Ballad of Songbirds and Snakes. I also participated in student short films in cooperation with the Wrocław Film School.

In my free time, I enjoy reading and currently work as a hotel receptionist.
